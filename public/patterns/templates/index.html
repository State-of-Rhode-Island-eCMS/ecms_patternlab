Executive Order 20-01

Advancing a 100% Renewable Energy Future for Rhode Island by 2030

January 17, 2020

Conduct an economic and energy market analysis and develop viable policy and programmatic pathways to meet one hundred percent (100%) of the state’s electricity demand with renewable energy resources by 2030 with a specific and implementable action plan to achieve this goal, due by December 31, 2020, which shall include recommended programmatic, legislative, and/or regulatory initiatives that can be advanced beginning in 2021.
